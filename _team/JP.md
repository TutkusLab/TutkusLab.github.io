<h2>About Justė</h2>
Justė joined our team during her bachelor studies, obtained BSc. degree in Molecular Biology (VU). Currently she is studying in MSc. Molecular biology programme (VU). Justė Paksaitė focuses on protein-DNA interaction studies at SM level, and also is highly skilled in protein expression, purification, genetic engineering, and cell work. During the MSc studies, Justė concentrated on researching Cas9 interactions with DNA in vivo. She defended her MSc thesis with the best grades and got "Magna cum laude" diploma on 2024-01. Then she left our team.
